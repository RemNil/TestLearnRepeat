Chapter 3 Justification

In later stages of clinical trialS, there is a well-established concept that can be invoked to justify research burdens to be accepted by human participants: “clinical equipoise” (Freedman 1987). In confirmatory research design of a randomized control trial…

In early phases, however, information is in general sparse and uncertainty about key dimensions is abundant. As such clinical equipoise needs to be established by earlier cinical development and cannot at the same time function as the justifcation for this very research.
